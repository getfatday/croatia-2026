01Mon

Brač Island — Skrip & Postira

Gate 1 Day 4 · Ferry from Split

Ferry to Brač Island. Visit historic Skrip — the oldest village on the island — and tour the Olive Oil Museum. Lunch at a local restaurant, then hike to Postira on the north coast.

🥾 Moderate 3.7 mi · 3 hrs · ±1,650 ft 🍽️ Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Aminess Vival Velaris, Supetar
02Tue

Vidova Gora & Zlatni Rat

Gate 1 Day 5 · Highest peak in the Adriatic islands

Hike from Podgazul Village up to Vidova Gora (778 m / 2,552 ft) — the highest mountain on any Adriatic island. Panoramic views, picnic lunch at the top.

Descend to explore Zlatni Rat (Golden Horn) Beach, the famous shape-shifting pebble spit. Wine tasting at a local household to close the day.

🥾 Moderate 4.3 mi · 3 hrs · ±755 ft 🍽️ Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Aminess Vival Velaris, Supetar
03Wed

Korčula — Marco Polo's island

Gate 1 Day 6 · Catamaran from Brač

Catamaran from Brač to Korčula. Hike from Pupnat through vineyards with archipelago views. Taste local products in Žrnovo Village.

Descend into Korčula Old Town — birthplace of Marco Polo. The walled town sits on a peninsula with views back to the Pelješac mainland.

🥾 Challenging 6.9 mi · 4 hrs · ±1,260 ft 🍽️ Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Aminess Korčula Heritage Hotel
04Thu

Mljet National Park

Gate 1 Day 7 · Day trip by catamaran

Catamaran to Mljet Island. Full-day exploration of the national park — hike through pine forests to discover two saltwater lakes (Veliko and Malo Jezero).

View Sveta Marija island with its 12th-century Benedictine monastery rising out of the lake. Dinner at a traditional restaurant.

🥾 Challenging 5.6 mi · 4 hrs 🍽️ Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 🏨 Aminess Korčula Heritage Hotel
05Fri

Dubrovnik Old Town

Gate 1 Day 8 · Walking tour + free afternoon

UNESCO Walking tour of Dubrovnik Old Town — Rector's Palace, Sponza Palace, Orlando Tower, Dominican Monastery, and the Franciscan Monastery with its 14th-century pharmacy (one of the oldest still in operation).

Free afternoon — option to walk the city walls independently for the iconic terracotta-roof view.

Walking the walls takes ~2 hours. Go in the late afternoon to avoid both the cruise crowds and the midday heat. Bring water.
🍽️ Breakfast, Dinner 🏨 Hotel Kompas / Valamar Lacroma / Hotel Lapad
06Sat

Kotor day trip (self-organized)

Gate 1 Day 9 is a leisure day

Optional Leave early (before 8 AM) for Kotor, Montenegro — about 2 to 2.5 hours by car or organized tour from Dubrovnik.

Walk the UNESCO old town and climb the fortress walls (San Giovanni / St. John's Fortress) for views over the Bay of Kotor. The full climb is 1,350 steps — go as far as you like. Lunch by the water, then return to Dubrovnik by evening.

Border note: Bring passports. US citizens don't need a visa. Border crossing can add 30–60 min each way — early June should be lighter than peak summer. Open question: car rental vs. organized day tour. Tour is easier (no parking, no border paperwork).
🍽️ Breakfast (Gate 1 included) · lunch & dinner on your own 🏨 Dubrovnik (Gate 1)
